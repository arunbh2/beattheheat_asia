The Silent Disaster Unfolding Now

Extreme heat is the most visible effect of climate change, yet it remains under-recognized and under-funded.

1.8B People in South Asia exposed to dangerous heat by 2030
480M People in India currently exposed to extreme heat conditions
7-8 Hours daily by 2050 when outdoor work becomes unsafe

The heat crisis is not a future problem; it is a silent disaster unfolding now.

Vulnerable communities who have historically contributed the least to current climate change are disproportionately affected. The inability to live, work, and learn in thermally safe environments is fundamentally an issue of human dignity and equity.
